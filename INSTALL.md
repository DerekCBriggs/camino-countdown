# How to Install Camino Countdown on Your Phone

Camino Countdown is a web app that can be installed on your phone just like a regular app. Once installed, it will appear on your home screen with its own icon and work even when you're offline.

## Step 1: Open the App in Your Browser

Visit: **https://derekcbriggs.github.io/camino-countdown/**

---

## Installing on iPhone (iOS)

1. **Open Safari** (this only works in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers)

2. **Visit the app URL** above

3. **Tap the Share button** at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up)

4. **Scroll down** and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**

5. **Name the app** (or keep the default "Camino Countdown") and tap **"Add"**

6. The app icon will now appear on your home screen!

---

## Installing on Android

1. **Open Chrome** (or your preferred browser)

2. **Visit the app URL** above

3. **Tap the menu** (three dots in the upper right corner)

4. **Tap "Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**

5. **Confirm** by tapping "Add" or "Install"

6. The app icon will now appear on your home screen!

---

## Using the App

Once installed:

- **Open the app** by tapping its icon on your home screen
- **Set your start date** by tapping the gear icon (settings) and entering when your Camino begins
- **View daily facts** about the Camino de Santiago — tap the fact to get a new random one!
- **Explore stages** by tapping "Explore the French Way Stages" to see all 33 stages from Saint Jean to Santiago with distances
- **Enjoy daily photos** — a curated Camino photo rotates each day
- **Works offline** - the countdown and facts work without internet

---

## Troubleshooting

**iPhone: "Add to Home Screen" not showing?**
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or another browser)
- Try refreshing the page first

**Android: No install option?**
- Try using Chrome browser
- Clear your browser cache and reload the page

**App not updating?**
- Close the app completely and reopen it
- Or visit the website in your browser to get the latest version
